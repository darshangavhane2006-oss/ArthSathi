Budget 2025 — Zero Tax Upto ₹12 Lakhs
Under the new regime, Section 87A rebate fully cancels your tax if total income is ≤ ₹12,00,000. This is the biggest tax relief in recent years.
Section 44ADA for Professionals
If you're a freelancer/consultant with income under ₹75L, you can declare 50% as taxable profit without maintaining books. Use ITR-4.
